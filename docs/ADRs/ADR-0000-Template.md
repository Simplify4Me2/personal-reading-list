## Decision

What is the decision we're making? State this as an active voice sentence using the "will" form.

Be specific about *what* we're choosing to do. If there are alternative approaches, acknowledge them briefly here or in the Alternatives section.

---

## Consequences

What becomes easier, harder, or changes as a result of this decision?

- **Positive outcomes:** What problems does this solve? What capabilities does it enable?
- **Negative outcomes:** What trade-offs are we accepting? What new problems might arise?
- **Dependencies:** What other decisions or changes does this commit us to?

Be honest about both upsides and downsides.
